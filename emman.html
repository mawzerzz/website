Challenges in My College Journey

One of the biggest challenges I’ve encountered in my college journey is the shift from my senior high school course to my current program. Back in senior high, I took the STEM track, which focused on subjects like science and math. Now, in college, I’m pursuing a degree in BSIT (Bachelor of Science in Information Technology), a field that is quite different from what I was used to. The transition hasn’t been easy, as I had to adjust to new concepts like programming, networks, and system analysis that weren’t really part of my STEM curriculum. It sometimes feels like I’m starting from scratch, and there are moments when I feel overwhelmed by the technicalities of my new course. But through it all, I’ve learned to be more patient with myself, realizing that this challenge is just part of my growth and that I have the ability to adapt, even when things feel unfamiliar.
